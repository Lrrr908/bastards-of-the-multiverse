Color Lab

Turn Bastone targets into real resin and filament.

The Color Lab is powered by BotMCMS. You can paste a Bastone code, a Pantone style number, a hex value, CMYK, RGB, or even a Behr or Benjamin Moore paint color. The Lab converts it to Lab, locks in a Bastone reference, and then finds the closest resins and filaments with delta E traffic lights.

Delta E numbers show in green, yellow, or red based on how close the match is.
Preview of the Lab

Bastone 123C example

  • Input: 123C, PMS 123C, hex, RGB, CMYK, or a paint chip name.
  • BotMCMS resolves it to Bastone 123C as the pure reference color.
  • Closest materials are pulled from curated resin and filament libraries with optional Amazon links.

How ordering works

Build colors online, finish the order in DMs.

The site is where you design. Instagram is where you and I talk. No automated cart, no auto charges. Just a clear project link and a human on the other end.

Project flow

From configuration to shipping

  1. You configure your figure, pick colors, and BotMCMS generates a unique project link.
  2. You DM that link to me on Instagram so we can confirm price, scope, and lead time.
  3. You pay via PayPal. The project page updates with payment received.
  4. As I print, clean up, paint, clear coat, pack, and ship, I update milestones on that same link.
  5. When it ships, the project page shows your tracking number and final photos of the finished bastard.
